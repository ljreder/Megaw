The Megaw Theatre List of Programs (1974-1986)

This page contains a list of programs used at the original Megaw Theatre that was open from 1974 to 1986 and located in Northridge, CA. The page is a temporary location for a partial collection of programs from shows produced back in this period. The programs where supplied by JoAnn Iaccino (formally JoAnna Mashall). JoAnn designed the original Megaw logo and many of the programs during this period. The programs where scanned and placed on this web page by Leonard Reder. Leonard spent a couple years at Megaw as a young man assisting with and learning about set construction and stage lighting.

Megaw Theatre opened it's doors originally on September 27, 1974 with the production of THE LITTLE FOXES. This was almost 50 years ago. It is my hope to grow this web page with production photographs and other information into a 50th. anniversary Megaw Theatre legacy page. The goal of the page is to preserve the memory for the many people that worked at Megaw Theatre.

Finally I would be remiss if I did not mension two people I personnally and foundly remember who have passed away and are no longer with us. First there is Sydney May Morrison (aka. Syd) the executive producer and founder of Megaw. Syd one summer day in 1974 interviewed me (a junior in high school) to particapate at the Megaw as a technical theatre apprentice which was the beginning of a couple years of learning. Syd was my advocate at Megaw and taught be about work. Dave Lukas, an extrodonary theatrical set designer and scenic carpetar, would be my first true mentor. I have life long memories of Dave and in 1976 working almost daily hammering out set after at that little theatre in Northridge. For the uninitiated you will notice these two individuals in every single program listed. With certainty everyone misses Syd and Dave who knew them at Megaw.

Leonard J. Reder
